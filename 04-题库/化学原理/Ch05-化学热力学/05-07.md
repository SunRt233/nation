# 5.7 利用以下各反应热，计算 (1)

## 题目

利用以下各反应热，计算 $\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{H}_4$ (1) 的生成焓和燃烧热。

(1) $2 \mathrm{NH}_{3}(\mathrm{~g}) + 3 \mathrm{~N}_{2} \mathrm{O}(\mathrm{~g}) \longrightarrow 4 \mathrm{~N}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + 3 \mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}(1)$ $\Delta H_{1}^{\ominus} = -1010 \mathrm{~kJ} \cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$   
(2) $\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{O}(\mathrm{g}) + 3\mathrm{H}_2(\mathrm{g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{H}_4(\mathrm{l}) + \mathrm{H}_2\mathrm{O}(\mathrm{l})$ $\Delta H_2^\ominus = -317\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$   
(3) $2\mathrm{NH}_{3}(\mathrm{g}) + \frac{1}{2}\mathrm{O}_{2}(\mathrm{g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm{N}_{2}\mathrm{H}_{4}(\mathrm{l}) + \mathrm{H}_{2}\mathrm{O}(\mathrm{l})$ $\Delta H_{3}^{\ominus} = -143\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$   
(4) $\mathrm{H}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + \frac{1}{2}\mathrm{O}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm{H}_{2}\mathrm{O}(1)$ $\Delta H_{4}^{\ominus} = -286\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$

## 参考答案

求 $\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{H}_4(1)$ 的生成焓，即为求出下列（5）式的 $\Delta H_{\mathrm{f}}^{\circ}$

(5) $2\mathrm{H}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + \mathrm{N}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm{N}_{2}\mathrm{H}_{4}(\mathrm{l})$ $\Delta H_{5}^{\ominus} = \Delta H_{1}^{\ominus}(\mathrm{N}_{2}\mathrm{H}_{4},\mathrm{l})$

$3\times (2)$ 式一（1）式 $+$ （3）式一（4）式，可得（6）式，即

(6) $8\mathrm{H}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + 4\mathrm{N}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) \longrightarrow 4\mathrm{N}_{2}\mathrm{H}_{4}(\mathrm{l})$

$$
\begin{array}{l} 3 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {g}) + 9 \mathrm {H} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow 3 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4} (\mathrm {l}) + 3 \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {l}) \quad (\Delta H ^ {\ominus}) ^ {\prime} = 3 \Delta H _ {2} ^ {\ominus} \\ 4 \mathrm {N} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + 3 \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {l}) \longrightarrow 2 \mathrm {N H} _ {3} (\mathrm {g}) + 3 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {g}) \quad (\Delta H ^ {\ominus}) ^ {\prime \prime} = - \Delta H _ {1} ^ {\ominus} \\ 2 \mathrm {N H} _ {3} (\mathrm {g}) + \frac {1}{2} \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4} (\mathrm {l}) + \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {l}) \quad (\Delta H ^ {\ominus}) ^ {\prime \prime} = \Delta H _ {3} ^ {\ominus} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
+) \quad \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (1) \longrightarrow \mathrm {H} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + \frac {1}{2} \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \quad (\Delta H ^ {\ominus}) ^ {\prime \prime \prime} = - \Delta H _ {4} ^ {\ominus}
$$

$$
(6) 8 \mathrm {H} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + 4 \mathrm {N} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow 4 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4} (\mathrm {l}) \quad \Delta H _ {6} ^ {\ominus} = ?
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta H _ {6} ^ {\ominus} = 3 \Delta H _ {2} ^ {\ominus} - \Delta H _ {1} ^ {\ominus} + \Delta H _ {3} ^ {\ominus} - \Delta H _ {4} ^ {\ominus} \\ = [ 3 \times (- 3 1 7) - (- 1 0 1 0) + (- 1 4 3) - (- 2 8 6) ] k J \cdot m o l ^ {- 1} \\ = 2 0 2 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\Delta H _ {1} ^ {\ominus} \left(\mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4}, 1\right) = \frac {1}{4} \Delta H _ {6} ^ {\ominus} = \frac {1}{4} \times 2 0 2 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} = 5 0. 5 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

求 $\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{H}_4$ (1) 的燃烧热，即求下列燃烧反应的 $\Delta H^{\ominus}$

$$
\mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4} (\mathrm {l}) + \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {N} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + 2 \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {l}) \quad [ \text {此 式} = 2 \times (4) \text {式} - (5) \text {式} ]
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta H _ {\text {燃}} ^ {\ominus} = 2 \times \Delta H _ {\mathrm {f}} ^ {\ominus} (\mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O}, \mathrm {l}) - \Delta H _ {\mathrm {f}} ^ {\ominus} (\mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {4}, \mathrm {l}) \\ = [ 2 \times (- 2 8 5. 8) - 5 0. 5 ] k J \cdot m o l ^ {- 1} = - 6 2 2. 1 k J \cdot m o l ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$
