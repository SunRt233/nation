# 5.8 е·ІзҹҘеңЁж—¶пјҢзҡ„ пјҢзҡ„ пјҢдё”ж №жҚ®дёҠ

## йўҳзӣ®

е·ІзҹҘеңЁ $298\mathrm{K}$ ж—¶пјҢ $\mathrm{CH_4(g)}$ зҡ„ $\Delta H_{\mathrm{f}}^{\ominus} = -74.6\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$ пјҢ $\mathrm{C}_2\mathrm{H}_6(\mathrm{g})$ зҡ„ $\Delta H_{\mathrm{f}}^{\ominus} = -84.0$ $\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$ пјҢдё”

$$
\mathrm {C} (\text {зҹі еўЁ}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {C} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H ^ {\ominus} = 7 1 6. 7 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\mathrm {H} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow 2 \mathrm {H} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H ^ {\ominus} = 4 3 6 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

## еҸӮиҖғзӯ”жЎҲ

в‘  $\mathrm{C}$ пјҲзҹіеўЁпјү $+2\mathrm{H}_{2}(\mathrm{g})\longrightarrow \mathrm{CH}_{4}(\mathrm{g})$ $\Delta H_{1}^{\ominus} = \Delta H_{1}^{\ominus}(\mathrm{CH}_{4},\mathrm{g}) = -74.6\mathrm{kJ}\cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} 2 \mathrm {C} (\text {зҹі еўЁ}) + 3 \mathrm {H} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {C} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {6} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H _ {2} ^ {\ominus} = \Delta H _ {\mathrm {f}} ^ {\ominus} \left(\mathrm {C} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {6}, \mathrm {g}\right) = - 8 4. 0 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \text {в‘ў} \mathrm {C} (\text {зҹі еўЁ}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {C} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H _ {3} ^ {\ominus} = 7 1 6. 7 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \Delta H _ {4} ^ {\ominus} = 4 3 6 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$

в‘ўејҸдёҖв‘ ејҸ $+2 \times 4$ ејҸпјҢеҫ—

$$
\mathrm {C H} _ {4} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {C} (\mathrm {g}) + 4 \mathrm {H} (\mathrm {g})
$$

$$
\Delta H ^ {\ominus} = \Delta H _ {3} ^ {\ominus} - \Delta H _ {1} ^ {\ominus} + 2 \times \Delta H _ {4} ^ {\ominus} = 1 6 6 3 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} = 4 \mathrm {E B (C - H)}
$$

$$
\mathrm {E B (C - H)} = \frac {1}{4} \Delta H ^ {\ominus} = 4 1 6 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$2\times 3$ ејҸдёҖ $в‘Ў$ ејҸ $+3\times 4$ ејҸпјҢеҫ—

$$
\mathrm {C} _ {2} \mathrm {H} _ {6} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow 2 \mathrm {C} (\mathrm {g}) + 6 \mathrm {H} (\mathrm {g})
$$

$$
\left(\Delta H ^ {\ominus}\right) ^ {\prime} = 2 \times \Delta H _ {3} ^ {\ominus} - \Delta H _ {2} ^ {\ominus} + 3 \times \Delta H _ {4} ^ {\ominus} = 2 8 2 5 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\left(\Delta H ^ {\ominus}\right) ^ {\prime} = \mathrm {E B (C - C)} + 6 \mathrm {E B (C - H)}
$$

$$
\mathrm {E B} (\mathrm {C} - \mathrm {C}) = \left(\Delta H ^ {\ominus}\right) ^ {\prime} - 6 \mathrm {E B} (\mathrm {C} - \mathrm {H}) = (2 8 2 5 - 6 \times 4 1 6) \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} = 3 2 9 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$
