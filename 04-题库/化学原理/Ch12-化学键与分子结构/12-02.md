# 12.2 Born-Haber循环计算晶格能

## 题目

试由以下数据画出 Born-Haber 循环，并计算氯化钾的晶格能 $(\mathrm{kJ} \cdot \mathrm{mol}^{-1})$ 。

$$
\mathrm {K (s)} \longrightarrow \mathrm {K (g)} \quad \Delta H _ {1} = 8 9. 0 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\mathrm {C l} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow 2 \mathrm {C l} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H _ {2} = 2 4 3 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\mathrm {K (g)} \longrightarrow \mathrm {K} ^ {+} (\mathrm {g}) + \mathrm {e} \quad \Delta H _ {3} = 4 1 9 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\mathrm {C l} (\mathrm {g}) + \mathrm {e} \longrightarrow \mathrm {C l} ^ {-} (\mathrm {g}) \quad \Delta H _ {4} = - 3 4 9 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\mathrm {K (s)} + \frac {1}{2} \mathrm {C l} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \longrightarrow \mathrm {K C l (s)} \quad \Delta H _ {5} = - 4 3 6. 5 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}
$$

## 参考答案

![[mineru/03-教材书籍/普通化学原理（第4版）/普通化学原理（第4版）习题解析_images/2cbfda50a425deac1d5bf3e7b0c8d1d52db430295c60edf37345fa65673d2e49.jpg]]

$\Delta H_{1} + \frac{1}{2}\Delta H_{2} + \Delta H_{3} + \Delta H_{4} + \Delta H = \Delta H_{5}$ 即

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta H _ {1} + \frac {1}{2} \Delta H _ {2} + \Delta H _ {3} + \Delta H _ {4} + (- U) = \Delta H _ {5} \\ U = \Delta H _ {1} + \frac {1}{2} \Delta H _ {2} + \Delta H _ {3} + \Delta H _ {4} - \Delta H _ {5} \\ = \left[ 8 9. 0 + \frac {1}{2} \times 2 4 3 + 4 1 9 + (- 3 4 9) - (- 4 3 6. 5) \right] k J \cdot m o l ^ {- 1} \\ = 7 1 7 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$
