# 14.16 Cu(OH)2沉淀判断

## 题目

某溶液中含 $0.10 \mathrm{~mol} \cdot \mathrm{dm}^{-3}$ 的游离 $\mathrm{NH}_{3} \cdot \mathrm{H}_{2} \mathrm{O}, 0.10 \mathrm{~mol} \cdot \mathrm{dm}^{-3}$ 的 $\mathrm{NH}_{4} \mathrm{Cl}$ 和 $0.15 \mathrm{~mol} \cdot \mathrm{dm}^{-3}$ 的 $[\mathrm{Cu}(\mathrm{NH}_{3})_{4}]^{2+}$ 。用计算说明有无 $\mathrm{Cu}(\mathrm{OH})_{2}$ 沉淀生成。[Cu(OH)2的 $K_{\mathrm{sp}} = 2.6 \times 10^{-19}$ 。]

## 参考答案

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} + 4 \mathrm {N H} _ {3} \rightleftharpoons \mathrm {C u} (\mathrm {N H} _ {3}) _ {4} ^ {2 +} \quad K _ {\text {稳}} = 2. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {1 3}, \quad \text {设} [ \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} ] = x \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \\ \frac {\left[ \mathrm {C u} \left(\mathrm {N H} _ {3}\right) _ {4} ^ {2 +} \right]}{\left[ \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} \right] \left[ \mathrm {N H} _ {3} \right] ^ {4}} = \frac {0 . 1 5}{x (0 . 1 0) ^ {4}} = 2. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {1 3}, x = 7. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {- 1 1}, \left[ \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} \right] = 7. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {- 1 1} \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathrm {N H} _ {3} + \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} \rightleftharpoons \mathrm {N H} _ {4} ^ {+} + \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} \quad K _ {\mathrm {b}} = 1. 8 \times 1 0 ^ {- 5}, \quad \text {设} [ \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} ] = y \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \\ \frac {\left[ \mathrm {N H} _ {4} ^ {+} \right] \left[ \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} \right]}{\left[ \mathrm {N H} _ {3} \right]} = \frac {0 . 1 0 y}{0 . 1 0} = 1. 8 \times 1 0 ^ {- 5}, \quad y = 1. 8 \times 1 0 ^ {- 5}, \left[ \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} \right] = 1. 8 \times 1 0 ^ {- 5} \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \\ Q = \left[ \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} \right] \left[ \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} \right] ^ {2} = 7. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {- 1 1} \times (1. 8 \times 1 0 ^ {- 5}) ^ {2} \\ = 2. 3 \times 1 0 ^ {- 2 0} <   K _ {\mathrm {s p}} (\mathrm {C u} (\mathrm {O H}) _ {2}), \text {故 不 能 生 成} \mathrm {C u} (\mathrm {O H}) _ {2} \text {沉 淀 。} \\ (= 2. 6 \times 1 0 ^ {- 1 9}) \\ \end{array}
$$
