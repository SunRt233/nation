# 10.17 дёӨз§Қж–№жі•жұӮе№іиЎЎеёёж•°

## йўҳзӣ®

иҜ•з”ЁдёӨз§Қж–№жі•и®Ўз®—дёӢеҲ—ж°§еҢ–иҝҳеҺҹеҸҚеә”зҡ„е№іиЎЎеёёж•°пјҡ

$$
6 \mathrm {C u I} + 4 \mathrm {N O} _ {3} ^ {-} + 1 6 \mathrm {H} ^ {+} = 6 \mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} + 3 \mathrm {I} _ {2} + 4 \mathrm {N O} + 8 \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O}
$$

## еҸӮиҖғзӯ”жЎҲ

и§Ј1пјҡжҹҘжңүе…іеҗ„зү©зҡ„ $\Delta G_{\mathrm{f}}^{\ominus}$ пјҢи®Ўз®— $\Delta_{\mathrm{r}}G^{\ominus}$ пјҢеҶҚз”ұ $\Delta_{\mathrm{r}}G^{\ominus}$ жұӮ $K^{\ominus}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta_ {\mathrm {r}} G ^ {\ominus} = \left[ 8 \times (- 2 3 7. 1 3) + 6 \times 6 5. 5 + 4 \times 8 7. 6 - 4 \times (- 1 1 1. 3) - 6 \times (- 6 9. 5) \right] \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ = - 2 9 1. 4 \mathrm {k J} \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\Delta_ {\mathrm {r}} G ^ {\ominus} = - 2. 3 0 3 R T \lg K ^ {\ominus}
$$

$$
\lg K ^ {\ominus} = - \frac {\Delta_ {r} G ^ {\ominus}}{2 . 3 0 3 R T} = \frac {2 9 1 . 4 \times 1 0 0 0 J \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1}}{2 . 3 0 3 \times 8 . 3 1 4 J \cdot \mathrm {m o l} ^ {- 1} \cdot \mathrm {K} ^ {- 1} \times 2 9 8 . 1 6 \mathrm {K}} = 5 1. 0 4, K ^ {\ominus} = 1. 1 \times 1 0 ^ {5 1}
$$

и§Ј2пјҡжҠҠдёҠиҝ°еҸҚеә”еҲҶи§ЈжҲҗиӢҘе№ІеҲҶеҸҚеә”д№Ӣе’ҢпјҢеҶҚз”ұеҲҶеҸҚеә”жұӮжҖ»еҸҚеә”зҡ„ $K^{\ominus}$

в‘  $\mathrm{CuI} \rightleftharpoons \mathrm{Cu}^{+} + \mathrm{I}^{-}$ $K_{1}^{\ominus} = K_{\mathrm{sp}} = 1.27 \times 10^{-12}$   
в‘Ў $3\mathrm{Cu}^{+} + \mathrm{NO}_{3}^{-} + 4\mathrm{H}^{+}\rightleftharpoons 3\mathrm{Cu}^{2 + } + \mathrm{NO} + 2\mathrm{H}_{2}\mathrm{O}$

$$
\lg K _ {2} ^ {\ominus} = \frac {n E _ {\text {жұ }} ^ {\ominus}}{0 . 0 5 9 2} = \frac {3 \times (0 . 9 5 7 - 0 . 1 5 3) \mathrm {V}}{0 . 0 5 9 2 \mathrm {V}} = 4 0. 7, K _ {2} ^ {\ominus} = 5 \times 1 0 ^ {4 0}
$$

в‘ў $6\mathrm{I}^{-} + 2\mathrm{NO}_{3}^{-} + 4\mathrm{H}^{+}\rightleftharpoons 3\mathrm{I}_{2} + 2\mathrm{NO} + 4\mathrm{H}_{2}\mathrm{O}$

$$
\lg K _ {3} ^ {\ominus} = \frac {6 \times (0 . 9 5 7 - 0 . 5 3 6) \mathrm {V}}{0 . 0 5 9 2 \mathrm {V}} = 4 2. 7, K _ {3} ^ {\ominus} = 5 \times 1 0 ^ {4 2}
$$

жҖ»еҸҚеә” $= 6\times 1$ ејҸ $+2\times 2$ ејҸ $+$ в‘ўејҸпјҢеҲҷ

$$
K ^ {\ominus} = \left(K _ {1} ^ {\ominus}\right) ^ {6} \times \left(K _ {2} ^ {\ominus}\right) ^ {2} \times K _ {3} ^ {\ominus} = 5 \times 1 0 ^ {5 2}
$$
