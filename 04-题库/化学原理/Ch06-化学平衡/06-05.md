# 6.5 多重平衡常数求算

## 题目

已知下列反应的平衡常数，试求 $298\mathrm{K}$ 时反应 $2\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{O}(\mathrm{g}) + 3\mathrm{O}_2(\mathrm{g}) \rightleftharpoons 2\mathrm{N}_2\mathrm{O}_4(\mathrm{g})$ 的 $K_{c} = ? K_{p}^{\mathrm{bar}} = ?$

(1) $\mathrm{N}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + \frac{1}{2}\mathrm{O}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) \rightleftharpoons \mathrm{N}_{2}\mathrm{O}(\mathrm{~g})$ $K_{c_1}(298\mathrm{K}) = 3.4\times 10^{-18}$

(2) $\mathrm{N}_{2} \mathrm{O}_{4}(\mathrm{~g}) \rightleftharpoons 2 \mathrm{NO}_{2}(\mathrm{~g})$ $K_{c_{2}}(298 \mathrm{~K}) = 4.6 \times 10^{-3}$

(3) $\frac{1}{2} \mathrm{~N}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) + \mathrm{O}_{2}(\mathrm{~g}) = \mathrm{NO}_{2}(\mathrm{~g})$ $K_{c_{3}}(298 \mathrm{~K}) = 4.1 \times 10^{-9}$

## 参考答案

$4\times (3)$ 式 $-2\times (1)$ 式 $-2\times (2)$ 式，或 $4\times (3)$ 式 $+2\times (1)$ 式的逆反应式 $+2\times (2)$ 式的逆反应式，即得所求式。

$$
2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + 4 \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \rightleftharpoons 4 \mathrm {N O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \quad K _ {c} ^ {\prime} = K _ {c _ {3}} ^ {4}
$$

$$
2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {g}) \rightleftharpoons 2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) + \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \quad K _ {c} ^ {\prime \prime} = 1 / K _ {c _ {1}} ^ {2}
$$

+）

$$
4 \mathrm {N O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \rightleftharpoons 2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} _ {4} (\mathrm {g}) \quad K _ {c} ^ {\prime \prime \prime} = 1 / K _ {c _ {2}} ^ {2}
$$

$$
2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} (\mathrm {g}) + 3 \mathrm {O} _ {2} (\mathrm {g}) \rightleftharpoons 2 \mathrm {N} _ {2} \mathrm {O} _ {4} (\mathrm {g})
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} K _ {c} = K _ {c} ^ {\prime} K _ {c} ^ {\prime \prime} K _ {c} ^ {\prime \prime \prime} = \frac {K _ {c _ {3}} ^ {4}}{K _ {c _ {1}} ^ {2} K _ {c _ {2}} ^ {2}} = \frac {(4 . 1 \times 1 0 ^ {- 9}) ^ {4}}{(3 . 4 \times 1 0 ^ {- 1 8} \times 4 . 6 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3}) ^ {2}} = 1. 2 \times 1 0 ^ {6} \\ K _ {p} ^ {\mathrm {b a r}} = K _ {c} (R T) ^ {\Delta n} = 1. 2 \times 1 0 ^ {6} \times (0. 0 8 3 \times 2 9 8) ^ {- 3} = 7 9 \\ \end{array}
$$
