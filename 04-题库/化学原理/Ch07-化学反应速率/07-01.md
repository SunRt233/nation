# 7.1 反应速率计量关系

## 题目

现有化学反应 $\mathrm{S}_2\mathrm{O}_8^{2-} + 3\mathrm{I}^- \longrightarrow 2\mathrm{SO}_4^{2-} + \mathrm{I}_3^-$ ，当反应速率 $\frac{-\mathrm{d}c(\mathrm{S}_2\mathrm{O}_8^{2-})}{\mathrm{d}t} = 2.0 \times 10^{-3}$ mol·dm⁻³·s⁻¹时，那么 $-\frac{\mathrm{d}c(\mathrm{I}^-)}{\mathrm{d}t} = ?$ $\frac{\mathrm{d}c(\mathrm{SO}_4^{2-})}{\mathrm{d}t} = ?$

## 参考答案

$\mathrm{S}_2\mathrm{O}_8^{2-} + 3\mathrm{I}^- \longrightarrow 2\mathrm{SO}_4^{2-} + \mathrm{I}_3^-$

$$
- \frac {\mathrm {d} c \left(\mathrm {S} _ {2} \mathrm {O} _ {8} ^ {2 -}\right)}{\mathrm {d} t} = - \frac {1}{3} \frac {\mathrm {d} c (\mathrm {I} ^ {-})}{\mathrm {d} t} = \frac {1}{2} \frac {\mathrm {d} c \left(\mathrm {S O} _ {4} ^ {2 -}\right)}{\mathrm {d} t} = \frac {\mathrm {d} c \left(\mathrm {I} _ {3} ^ {-}\right)}{\mathrm {d} t}
$$

当 $-\frac{\mathrm{dc}(\mathrm{S}_2\mathrm{O}_8^{2-})}{\mathrm{dt}} = 2.0 \times 10^{-3} \mathrm{~mol} \cdot \mathrm{dm}^{-3} \cdot \mathrm{s}^{-1}$ 时，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} - \frac {\mathrm {d} c (\mathrm {I} ^ {-})}{\mathrm {d} t} = 3 \times \frac {- \mathrm {d} c (\mathrm {S} _ {2} \mathrm {O} _ {8} ^ {2 -})}{\mathrm {d} t} = 3 \times (2. 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3}) \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \cdot \mathrm {s} ^ {- 1} \\ = 6. 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \cdot \mathrm {s} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} c \left(\mathrm {S O} _ {4} ^ {2 -}\right)}{\mathrm {d} t} = 2 \times \frac {- \mathrm {d} c \left(\mathrm {S} _ {2} \mathrm {O} _ {8} ^ {2 -}\right)}{\mathrm {d} t} = 2 \times (2. 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3}) \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \cdot \mathrm {s} ^ {- 1} \\ = 4. 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {d m} ^ {- 3} \cdot \mathrm {s} ^ {- 1} \\ \end{array}
$$
